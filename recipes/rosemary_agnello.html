Roast Lamb with Rosemary / Agnello al Rosmarino

Ingredients
  • 1/2 leg of lamb
  • 2 to 3 garlic cloves, cut lengthways into thin slivers
  • 4 springs of fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • 7 Tbsps olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 fresh leaves of sage, chopped
Directions

Preheat the oven to 400Â°F

Using the point of a sharp knife make deep
incisions in the lamb,especially near the bone,
and insert the slivers of garlic.

Put the lamb in a roasting pan rub it over with
3 Tbsps of olive oil

Sprinkle the chopped rosemary and sage, patting it
firmly, and season with salt and pepper.

Roast for 30 minutes, turn over once.

Lower the oven to 375f. Turn the lamb over again.

Roast for a further 1 1/2 hours until the lamb is tender.

Transfer the lamb to a carving board, tent with foil and
leave in a warm place for 10 minutes so that the flesh firms
for easier carving..

Serve whole or carved into thin slices
