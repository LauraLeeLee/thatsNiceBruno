Meatballs/Polpette

Ingredients
  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 Tbs chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 Tbsp grated Parmigiano cheese
  • 3 slices day old Italian bread
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
Directions

Put the ground beef in a large bowl

Add eggs, parsley, salt and pepper, cheese and lemon juice

Wet the bread, squeeze out the water and shred it into the meat mixture.
{If the crusts are to crusty and donâ€™t shred easily, discard them)

Mix everything well but not too harshly. From the mixture in ball about the size of a large egg.

Put the meat balls in a cooking sheet and place in the oven, cook them until
they are nicely browned and crisp on both sides.

Remove them and drain on a paper towel

You can serve with wedges of lemon or simmer in a tomato sauce
for about 15 to 20 minutes more.
