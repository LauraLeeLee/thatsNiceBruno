Panzanella Insalata

Ingredients
    For Salad:
  • 3 1/2 cups stale bread white or brow or rolls
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled and mince
  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 1 large red onions, or 6 spring onions
  • 3 â€“ 4 leaves fresh basil- to garnish
    • For Dressing:
  • 4 Tbsps extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 Tbsps white wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Directions

Cut the bread or rolls into thick slices.

Place in a shallow bowl, and soak with cold water.

Leave for at least 30 minutes

Cut the tomatoes into chunks. Place in a serving bowl.

Finely slice the onion or spring onion, and add them to the tomatoes.

Squeeze as much water out the bread as possible, and add it to the vegetables

Make a dressing with the oil and vinegar.

Season with salt and pepper.

Pour it over the salad and mix well.

decorate with the basil leaves, Allow to stand in a cool place for at least 2 hours before serving
