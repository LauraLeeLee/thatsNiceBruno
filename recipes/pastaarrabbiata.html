Pasta Arrabbiata

Ingredients
  • 1 lb chopped tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2/3 cup dry white wine
  • 1 Tbsp tomato pureeâ€™
  • 1 pinch crushed red hot pepper
  • 11 oz penne or rigatoni
  • 1 tbsp fresh chopped parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Pecorino cheese to serve
Directions

Put the tomatoes, garlic, wine,tomato puree and hot pepper
in a saucepan and bring to the boil.

Cover and simmer gently. Add parsley.

Taste for seasoning.

If you prefer a hotter taste, add more crush hot pepper.

Drain the pasta and tip into a large bowl.

Pour the sauce over the pasta and toss to mix.

Serve at once, sprinkled with grated Pecorino
and the remaining parsley.
