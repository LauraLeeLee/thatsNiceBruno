Tiramisu

Ingredients
  • 1 pound mascarpone
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoon amaretto or Cognac
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 24 savoiardi (Italian ladyfingers)
  • 1 cup brewed espresso at room temperature
  • ½ cup chopped bittersweet chocolate
Directions
  • In a large bowl, whisk together the mascarpone, sugar and amaretto until smooth.
  • In a chilled bowl with chilled beaters whip the cream until soft.
  • Fold the cream into the mascarpone mixture.
  • Lightly dip half of savoiardi in the espresso and arrange in a single layer in the bottom of an 8 inch square pan.
  • Lightly dip half of savoiardi in the espresso and arrange in a single layer in the bottom of an 8 inch square pan.
  • Sprinkle with half the chocolate.
  • Dip the remaining savoiardi in the espresso.
  • Top with the remaining mascarpone mixture, spreading it smooth.
  • Sprinkle with the remaining chocolate.
  • Cover with wrap.
  • Refrigerate for several hours or overnight before serving.
