Pizza Dough

Ingredients
  • 5 cups unbleached flour
  • 2 packets active dry yeast
  • 2 cups warm water
  • olive oil
Directions
  • Add just a bit of warm water to the yeast and start stirring it with a fork.
  • As it gathers flour and a paste start to form, add more water.
  • Keep on stirring and adding water to the paste until all the water is used.
  • Start to work it with your hands.
  • At first the dough seems hopeless and very raggedy. But in a about 2 minutes it take on the tough shape and consistency of play dough, and will continue to pick up the remaining flour as you knead back and forth.
  • Add the salt. Keep on kneading until the dough is soft, smooth and warm.
  • At the end of about 15 minutes you should have a very smooth ball, so light in texture you can see your hadprint briefly before the dough bounces back
  • If, after all the original flour has been used, the dough begins to stick to the counter, sprinkle with some extra flour.
  • Put a teaspoon or so of olive oil in a bowl at last twice as big as the finished ball of dough. Roll the dough around in the oil until it is well coated.
  • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, sealing it well.
  • Put in a warm place and let it rise until doubled in size.
  • Preheat the oven to 450f.
  • Punch the dough down, cut in fourths, and flatten each piece out on a floured surface,Roll the pieces.
  • Bake at 450 f. the time depending on the type of pizza.
  • Usually it take about 20 minutes total timing to get a well baked pizza, one that's toasty and crispy on the bottom and edges.
