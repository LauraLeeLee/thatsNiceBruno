Gelato Alla Panna

Ingredients
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tspn vanilla extract
  • 3 slices day old Italian bread
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
Directions

Combine milk, cream, and sugar in top of a double boiler;
heat until small bubbles form around sides of the pan.

Add small amount of cream mixture to eggs yolks,
then stir yolks into cream mixture in top of double boiler.

Cook over simmering water, stirring constantly,
until mixture thickens enough to lightly coat a metal spoon.

Add vanilla. Strain, cool thoroughly.

Pour into two deep metal freezer trays, cover wit foil.

Freeze until firm but not hard.

Chill bowl and beater.

Beat with electric mixer on high speed until double in volume.

Return to tray, freeze until firm but not hard.

Break up, beat again.

Freeze until firm but not hard.

Repeat, freeze until firm for 2 hours.
