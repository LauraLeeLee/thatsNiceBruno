Spaghetti alla Puttanesca

Ingredients
  • 4 Tbsps olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic,finely chopped
  • 12 oz tomatoes, fresh or canned, chopped
  • pinch of crushed hot pepper
  • 2 oz anchovy fillets, chopped
  • 4 oz pitted black olives
  • 2 Tbsps capers, rinsed
  • 1 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 14 oz spaghetti
  • 2 Tbsps chopped fresh parsley to serve
  • salt and pepper to taste
Directions

Bring a large pan of water to the boil.

Heat the oil in a large pan.
Add the garlic and the dried hot pepper, and cook
for 2 to 3 minutes until the garlic is golden.

Add the anchovys, and mash them into the garlic with a fork.

Add the tomatoes, olives, capers and tomato paste.

Stir well cook over moderate heat.

Add salt to the boiling water, and put in the spaghetti.

Stir, and cook until the pasta is all dente, Drain.

Turn the spaghetti into the sauce.
Raise the heat, and cook for 1 to 2 minutes, turning the pasta constantly.

Sprinkle with parsley if desired and serve.

Traditionally no cheese is served with this sauce.
