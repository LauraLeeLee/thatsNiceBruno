Tuna and Bean Salad/ Tonno e Fagioli

Ingredients
  • 2- 14 oz cans Cannellini or Bordoli beans
  • 2- 7 oz cans tuna fish, drained
  • 4- Tbsps extra virgin olive oil
  • 2- Tbsps fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 Tbsp fresh chopped parsley
  • 3 spring onions, thinkly sliced
Directions

Pour the beans into a large strainer
and rinse under cold water. Drain well.
Place in a serving dish.

Break the tuna into fairly large flakes and
arrange over the beans.

In a small bowl make the dressing by combining
the oil with the lemon juice.

Season with salt and pepper, and stir in the parsley.

Mix well. Pour over the beans and tuna.

Sprinkle with the spring onions.

Toss well before serving.
