Ricotta Cheesecake

Ingredients
  • 2 cups low fat ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup double cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 cup caster sugar
  • finely grated ring of 1 orange
  • finely grated ring of 1 lemon
  • For the pastry
  • 1 1/2 cup plain flour
  • 3 Tbsps caster sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 8 Tbsps chilled butter, diced
  • 1 egg yolk
Directions

    Make the pastry:

  • Sift the flour, sugar and salt on to a work surface.
  • Make a well in the center and put in the diced butter and eggs yolk.
  • Gradually work the flour into the diced butter and eggs yolk, using your fingertips.
  • Gather the dough together, reserve about a quarter for the lattice, then press the rest into a 9 inch tin with a removable base.
  • Chill the pastry case for 30 minutes.

    • Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 375f.and make the filling:

  • Put all the ricotta, cream, eggs, egg yolk, sugar and orange and lemon rinds in a large bowl and beat together until evenly mixed.
  • Prick the bottom of the pastry case, than line with foil and fill with baking beans.
  • Bake blind for 15 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack, remove the foil and beans and allow the tart shell to cool the tin.
  • Spoon the cheese and cream filling into the pastry case and level the surface.
  • Roll out the reserved dough and cut into strips.
  • Arrange the strips on the top of the filling in a lattice pattern, sticking them in place with water.
  • Bake for 30/35 minutes until golden and set.
  • Transfer to a wire rack and leave to cool, then carefully remove the side of the tin, leving the cheesecake on the tin base.
